Please note the standard dress codes and conditions of play that can be viewed here and available as a pdf here

These need to be followed, for example it states

Tournament standard dress is required (long sleeved collared shirt, bow tie (optional if a vest is worn), dress pants in a solid dark colour, dress shoes and socks). Waistcoat and bow tie is required from the quarter finals onwards. Failure to comply with these conditions will result in forfeiture of the match. 

All players are to report to the Tournament Director 15 minutes before their scheduled starting time, and are to be ready to play at this time.  

Good luck to all competitors and we hope you have a great tournament
