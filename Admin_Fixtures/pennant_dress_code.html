Pennant Dress Code
All players by taking part in pennant competition agree to the bylaws including the dress
code as below:
The minimum dress standard:
•
Trousers (including jeans), skirt or a dress
•
Polo shirt (preferably a club shirt), Collared shirt or Blouse
•
Covered footwear.
Under no circumstances shall any of the following articles of clothing be acceptable:
•
T-shirts, tracksuit tops
•
Tracksuit pants, Cargo pants, Shorts
•
Sandals, thongs
•
Dirty, torn, or damaged clothing
•
Clothing that could damage a table (such as trousers with large studs) or torn or
ripped clothing/footwear.
Penalty: incorrectly dressed players shall not be permitted to play
This is incumbent on the captains to enforce at the time of the breach.
